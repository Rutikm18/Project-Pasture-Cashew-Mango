हापूसबद्दल · About Hapus

India's Most
Celebrated Mango

भारताचा सर्वश्रेष्ठ आंबा — GI प्रमाणित

The Alphonso (हापूस) is the king of mangoes — rich, non-fibrous, intensely fragrant with a saffron-like aroma that fills the room. Grown only in the coastal Konkan belt of Maharashtra, particularly Ratnagiri and Devgad, it carries a Geographical Indication (GI) tag recognising its unique origin.

आमच्या रत्नागिरीच्या बागेत हे आंबे झाडावरच पिकतात. कोणतेही कार्बाइड (carbide) किंवा रासायनिक प्रक्रिया नाही. झाड-पका आंबा — हीच आमची ओळख.

Our family tends to these mango trees with the same care as our cashew orchards. Tree-ripened Hapus has a texture and sweetness that is simply impossible to replicate with forced ripening.

  • GI Certified Ratnagiri Hapus — authenticity guaranteed
  • Tree-ripened (झाड-पका) — naturally sweet, no carbide
  • Rich saffron-yellow flesh, non-fibrous, melt-in-mouth texture
  • Packed in ventilated wooden crates lined with dried leaves
  • Shipped within 24 hours of harvest — maximum freshness
GI प्रमाणपत्र · Why GI Matters

What Makes Ratnagiri Hapus
Genuine

रत्नागिरी हापूस खरा कसा ओळखावा
📍
Geographic Origin
भौगोलिक उत्पत्ती

Genuine Hapus comes only from the districts of Ratnagiri, Sindhudurg, and Devgad in Maharashtra. The unique combination of laterite soil, sea breeze (खारी हवा), and monsoon pattern cannot be replicated anywhere else.

🌳
Tree-Ripened Only
झाड-पका हापूस

True Hapus is allowed to ripen naturally on the tree for 120–130 days. When it falls on its own, it is harvested. This patience creates the intense sweetness and deep fragrance that carbide-ripened mangoes can never match.

🎨
The Saffron Glow
केशरी रंग आणि सुगंध

Authentic Ratnagiri Hapus has a deep saffron-yellow (केशरी) flesh, not the pale yellow of imitations. The fragrance alone — described as floral, honey-like, and intensely aromatic — distinguishes it from all other varieties.

विशेषता · What Makes It Special

From Our Orchard
to Your Table

आमच्या बागेतून थेट तुमच्या ताटात
01
🌸
December — Flowering (मोहोर)
डिसेंबर — आंब्याचा मोहोर

Mango trees begin to bloom with panicles of small cream-white flowers (मोहोर). Each cluster can produce 1–4 mangoes. We carefully monitor and manage the flowering to ensure good fruit set without any chemical interventions.

02
🌱
January–March — Growing Period
जानेवारी–मार्च — वाढीचा काळ

Tiny mango fruits grow slowly over 3 months. During this time, the sea breeze and Konkan sunshine work their magic on the developing mangoes, building the unique sugar profile and aroma compounds found in Ratnagiri Hapus.

03
🥭
April–May — Harvest (तोडणी)
एप्रिल–मे — आंबा तोडणी

When the mango naturally separates from the stem or is at perfect maturity, we harvest by hand. Each mango is gently wrapped in dried leaves or newspaper to prevent bruising, and arranged in wooden crates for transport.

04
📦
Packed & Dispatched in 24 Hours
२४ तासात पॅक करून पाठवणे

Harvested mangoes are graded (by size and ripeness), packed in wooden crates with ventilation holes, and dispatched within 24 hours. We never store in cold rooms — fresh from the tree, straight to your hands.
