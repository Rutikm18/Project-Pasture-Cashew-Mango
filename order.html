Why We Don't
Use Middlemen

"जेव्हा दुकानात काजू ₹1200 किलोला मिळतो, त्यापैकी आम्हाला फक्त ₹300 मिळायचे. बाकी सगळे दलाल खायचे."

"When cashews sold for ₹1200/kg in shops, we received only ₹300. Brokers took the rest. By selling directly to you, we earn fairly — and you pay less for fresher cashews. That is what this farm is about."

— Ramchandra Sawant, Ratnagiri

What Happens When You Order
01
Order Received
Your order arrives on our phone. We confirm within a few hours — sometimes, my son calls you himself.
02
Graded by Hand
Village women from our community sort the exact grade you ordered — W180, W210, or W240 — by eye and touch. This is skill passed through generations.
03
Vacuum Sealed & Labelled
Your cashews go into food-grade vacuum pouches, sealed to lock in crunch and freshness. Each pack is labelled by hand with the harvest date.
04
Shipped Within 48 Hours
We hand the parcel to the courier ourselves. From our farm gate to your kitchen door. No warehouse. No cold room. No middleman.
